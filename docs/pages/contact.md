# Contact

Contact is a site-wide page reached from Contact us in the nav. It gives people a direct way to
reach Grounded Art.

## Content

- A short heading and a line inviting inquiries.
- Our contact details: the email, and the takedown and opt-out contact already used in the
  footer.
- A form: name, email, subject, and message.

## Behaviour

The form posts to the API, which stores the message and notifies us. On success the user sees a
plain confirmation. On error the user is asked to try again or to email us directly.

## The re-skin

The mockup is a generic template. "Book Now", the placeholder Johannesburg address, and the
orange button are not ours. The action is "Send", the details are Grounded Art's, and the page
renders in the shared cream, black, and rust tokens with Noto Serif headings.

## Relationship to existing contact paths

The footer keeps its direct email and the takedown and opt-out contact. The galleries and
artists invitation on the landing stays a mailto in v1. The contact page is an additional,
general front door, not a replacement for those.
